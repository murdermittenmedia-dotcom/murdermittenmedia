- Platforms: Instagram, YouTube, Threads, Facebook

<idea>

**Design Movement:** Detroit Brutalism meets Street Press
**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, editorial energy — no polish for polish's sake
2. High-contrast black/red/white color blocking
3. Oversized typography as a structural element
4. Content-first layout with aggressive asymmetry

**Color Philosophy:** Deep matte black (#0A0A0A) as the canvas, blood red (#C8102E) as the pulse, and stark white for text. Red is used sparingly — only for numbers, accents, and the logo mark — making it feel dangerous and intentional.

**Layout Paradigm:** Newspaper-grid hybrid. The hero is a full-bleed dark panel with a massive stat counter. Below, content is arranged in an editorial grid — some cards full-width, some narrow columns — mimicking a tabloid layout.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Knife/blade divider lines between sections
2. Glitch-text animation on the hero headline
3. Red dot "LIVE" indicator next to stats

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal content like flipping a page. Clicks feel weighted and immediate.

**Animation:** Subtle glitch on hero text on load. Stats count up from 0. Cards slide in from left on scroll.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for display/headlines (all-caps, condensed), "Space Grotesk" for body copy. Hierarchy: 96px hero → 48px section titles → 16px body.

<idea>

**Design Movement:** Dark Editorial — Premium Street Media
**Core Principles:**
1. Cinematic darkness with surgical use of red and white
2. Large-scale numbers and stats as visual anchors
3. Asymmetric split layouts — text left, visual right
4. Motion-forward: everything enters with purpose

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#080808) background. Crimson red (#D10000) for accent elements, numbers, and the brand mark. Pure white (#FFFFFF) for primary text. A subtle dark gray (#1C1C1C) for card surfaces. This palette communicates power, credibility, and street authenticity.

**Layout Paradigm:** Split-panel hero (text/stat left, image right). Below: alternating full-bleed and contained sections. Stats section uses a dark card grid. Recent posts in a horizontal scroll strip.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Animated stat counters with red number color
2. Red left-border accent on featured quotes/captions
3. Subtle red glow on hover states

**Interaction Philosophy:** Clean, deliberate. Hover reveals more info. Scroll triggers entrance animations. Everything feels intentional — like the brand itself.

**Animation:** Stats count up on scroll-into-view. Hero text slides up on load. Cards fade in with slight upward drift.

**Typography System:** "Anton" for display headlines (bold, condensed, authoritative), "DM Sans" for body and UI text. Anton at 80-120px for hero, 40px for section headers.

## Selected Approach: Dark Editorial — Premium Street Media (Option 3)

This approach best captures Murder Mitten Media's identity: credible, powerful, street-authentic, and media-forward. The dark editorial aesthetic positions them as a serious media brand — not just an Instagram page — while the red accents and bold typography reflect the energy of Michigan rap culture.
